About EduCafé

EduCafe connects students and professors by allowing professors to host informal roundtable discussions on topics of their choice. We call these discussions EduMeets and believe that they allow for profound and intellectual connections to develop without the pressure of tense formality.

Across the United States, university students feel disenfranchised from interacting with professors who interest them at their universities because they aren't being taught by them or because these professors are too "famous". EduCafé presents an opportunity for students to interact with professors more casually while fulfilling a common desire among professors to have intimate intellectual discussions with students.
