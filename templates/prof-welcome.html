MORE ABOUT EDUMEETS

What is an EduMeet?

EduMeet might have various forms, but the idea is to connect Professors with students outside of classroom setting. EduMeets will allow professors to learn more about the interesting life-stories of the students and vice versa. The topic of discussion, number of students, location, and time can be chosen when posting an EduMeet, for example: Commons, Friday 2 PM, 4 students, Global warming.

How to post an EduMeet?

Click on Post and host button that you find higher on this page. You will then be asked to provide basic information about the EduMeet you will host (like location, date and time, number of students present, etc.). Once you submit this form it's all done! Congratulations you posted an EduMeet! We will send you a short bio of each student attending your EduMeet before it starts, so you can see if there is anything interesting about them you want to talk about.
