What is an EduMeet and how to register for one

What is an EduMeet?

EduMeet might have various forms, but the idea is to connect students with professors outside of classroom setting. EduMeets will allow students to learn more about the professor, his field of study, or his views on a particular subject. The professor hosting the EduMeet choses location and time, and also how many students can attend. They also have the option to chose a topic not to be missed during the conversation.

How to register for and attend an EduMeet?

Click on Browse and register button that you can find higher on this page. You will then be able to swipe through open EduMeets and register for them. Once you swipe through all of the new and available EduMeets, you will see an overview of all offered EduMeets and you can register for an EduMeet you didn't register for earlier, or you can cancel your previous registrations.
